# HeadChat

Deploy your chatbot to your website in seconds.

Once you have deployed the bot. Go to your [dashboard](https://console.recime.io) dashboard and copy the script from `publish->headchat` tab as shown below:  

![](headchat.png)


To get the messenger to appear on your web page, simply paste the snippet before the `</body>` tag. That's it, you should now see the `headchat` icon. Click on it to start chatting.


![](faqbot.png)


`HeadChat` support rich metadata enabling you to go beyond simple FAQs and bring in rich AI customer experience.
