#Setup your bot

First step is to setup the bot, you can do this by using the botfather app.

Search for the botfather app (this is will assist you to setup and manage your bot).

Type /help to see all available command:

Type or click on /newbot to create a new bot:

Follow the instructions and type a new name for your bot. I have picked Recime Bot as the name and recime_bot as its username.

Copy the access token and store it in a safe place like dropbox or notes which you can access from your mac / pc.
